about us

what makes our coffee special?

Nestled in the heart of New York, Home Coffee is more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee. It's a haven for coffee enthusiasts and a hub for our community. Founded in 2016, our coffee shop was born out of a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a warm, inviting space for everyone.

At Home Coffee, our mission is to deliver the finest coffee experience while fostering a sense of community. We believe that great coffee has the power to bring people together and create lasting memories
